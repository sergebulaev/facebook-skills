# The Facebook Page Pillar Framework

Content pillars keep a Page from drifting into all-promotion or all-filler. Pick
3-4 pillars, assign a rough weekly weight, and map each to the Facebook formulas
that serve it. The default mix below works for most business Pages; adjust to the
audience.

## The four default pillars

### 1. Value (default 40%)

Posts that teach, inform, or give the reader something usable. The backbone of a
Page that people follow on purpose.

- **Formulas:** FB1 One-Line Opinion, FB2 Tiny Number, FB7 Useful Tip
- **Primary goal:** shares (useful content gets passed on)
- **Example:** "Day-old bread? 10 min in a 180C oven brings it back."

### 2. Community (default 30%)

Posts that pull the audience into a conversation or put a customer in the
spotlight. This is where comments and shares come from.

- **Formulas:** FB3 Ask-the-Page, FB4 This-or-That, FB10 Community Spotlight
- **Primary goal:** comments and shares
- **Example:** "Croissant or cinnamon roll? Be honest."

### 3. Behind-the-Scenes (default 20%)

Posts that show the real people and process behind the Page. They build the trust
that makes the Value and Promotion posts land.

- **Formulas:** FB5 Relatable One-Liner, FB6 Behind-the-Scenes, FB8 Story Post
- **Primary goal:** reactions and shares
- **Example:** "Our baker named the sourdough starter Gary."

### 4. Promotion (default 10%)

Posts that directly sell or launch. Kept small on purpose: overuse kills reach
and trust. Lead human, never "We are thrilled to announce".

- **Formulas:** FB9 Announcement with Stakes, FB2 (your own data), FB10 (a result
  that implies the product)
- **Primary goal:** shares
- **Example:** "The spring menu drops Monday. Three new things."

## Rules for the mix

- **No pillar over 60% of the week.** A Page that is all Value reads as a blog; a
  Page that is all Community reads as needy.
- **Promotion is the smallest pillar.** 1-2 posts a week. The Value, Community,
  and Behind-the-Scenes posts are what earn the reach the Promotion posts borrow.
- **Lead short across every pillar.** The under-80-char post is the engagement
  sweet spot regardless of pillar. Story posts (FB8, FB10) are the deliberate
  exception.
- **Weight the week toward shares.** Shares are the reach multiplier on a Page,
  so the Value and Promotion pillars lean on share-goal formulas.

## Adapting the mix

| Page type | Suggested shift |
|---|---|
| New Page building an audience | more Community (50%) to seed comments and follows |
| Established Page with a loyal base | more Value (50%) to stay useful |
| Launch week | bump Promotion to 20%, but keep every promo post human |
| Local / brick-and-mortar | more Behind-the-Scenes (30%) for the human face |

The pillars are a discipline, not a cage. The weekly balance check in the
planner enforces the spread so no single pillar or goal takes over.
