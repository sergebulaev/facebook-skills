# Example Week (Facebook Page)

A filled-in 7-day plan for a local bakery launching a spring menu. Audience:
local families and the weekend brunch crowd. Pillar mix: 40% Value, 30%
Community, 20% Behind-the-Scenes, 10% Promotion. Cadence: 1 post/day.

## 7-day calendar

| Day | Type | Pillar | Formula | Angle | Goal | Time |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Mon | short | Value | FB2 Tiny Number | "Every loaf is mixed by hand. All 200 a day." | shares | 1:00 PM |
| Tue | short | Community | FB3 Ask-the-Page | "Sweet or savory: what is your go-to brunch order?" | comments | 12:30 PM |
| Wed | story | Behind-the-Scenes | FB6 Behind-the-Scenes | "Our baker named the sourdough starter Gary. Gary is 6 years old." | reactions | 1:00 PM |
| Thu | short | Value | FB7 Useful Tip | "Day-old bread? 10 min in a 180C oven brings it back to life." | shares | 1:30 PM |
| Fri | short | Community | FB4 This-or-That | "Croissant or cinnamon roll? Be honest." | comments | 12:00 PM |
| Sat | story | Community | FB10 Spotlight | "Maria mapped her whole shop launch on our planner. Sold out day one." | shares | 10:00 AM |
| Sun | short | Promotion | FB9 Announcement | "The spring menu drops Monday. Three new things, one returning favorite." | shares | 11:00 AM |

## Why this week works

- **Leads short.** 5 of 7 posts are short (aiming under 80 chars), the engagement
  workhorse. Only Wednesday and Saturday go long, and both earn it (a real
  behind-the-scenes moment and a customer spotlight).
- **Share-weighted.** 4 posts target shares (the reach multiplier): the number,
  the tip, the spotlight, the launch.
- **Comments built in.** Tuesday's question and Friday's this-or-that are
  low-effort comment-drivers placed mid-week and end-of-week.
- **A human face.** Wednesday's Gary-the-starter post and Saturday's spotlight
  keep the Page warm, not corporate.
- **Promotion held to one post.** The Sunday announcement leads human ("three new
  things, one returning favorite"), not "We are thrilled to announce".

## Daily comment-reply target

- **Every day:** clear the comment queue, ideally within the first hour of each
  post (replying fast lifts reach).
- **Mon/Thu (share posts):** use C4 Turn-It-Into-a-Share on the warm comments to
  make the threads more shareable.
- **Tue/Fri (question posts):** expect volume. Use C1 Answer-the-Question and
  keep it moving; reply to as many as you can in the first two hours.
- **Sat (spotlight):** use C5 Invite-the-Spotlight on anyone who shares their own
  story, to seed next week's FB10 post.
- **Any complaint:** C3 Handle-the-Complaint first, before anything else.
